Version: # + core15 or master (depending on which repo you downloaded from)
D2BS #: this number is on the top of your diablo 2, it will say D2BS 1.4.XXXX
Error Logs: if it is a d2bot crash, post the crash report if its a d2bs crash post your d2bs.log (located in d2bs folder)
Additional info: where did it happen, what were the circumstances, can you reproduce the error etc
